Learning Reflection

There were a lot of challenges when it comes to learning about Text Representation and Preprocessing. I find confusing to differentiate the two the first time. It got a little overwhelming at times to know what each and every step is for-to understand whether tokenization has a particular purpose or a stop word removal. Here, as each approach bears a different impact on performance, so it was a great challenge in choosing the appropriate one such as TF-IDF or word embeddings. Though sometimes it became hard to make sense out of the transformations on data, I came to learn that even small changes would have a great impact.
