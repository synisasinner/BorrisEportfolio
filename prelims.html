Overall Reflection

It was a great but scary yet thrilling experiencing the Prelimanary period. All the learning phases from choosing between TF-IDF and word embeddings to what tokenization is and which words are stop words presented challenges. I was a failure, I thought, every week because of these lessons, where I questioned my abilities each time through, and obsessed over just how to get the data to match. But for that reason, by going through all the many difficulties, I discovered what perseverance, practice, and even diligence mean. I am proud of myself for getting a good grade, although I am aiming for much more higher than that, this is a good start for me.
