Overall Reflection

During the midterm period, I had the opportunity to explore NLP applications with exploring different models like BERT, RoBERTa, ALBERT, and DistilBERT. I also learned about the Evaluation Metrics to test the model's performance. After comparing their merits and demerits, I was able to understand how different architectures impact on precision, speed, and effectiveness. After learning about these and other models, me and my team decided to work on what's close to us. To start within our roots so that it will be easier for us to gather the needed data for the project in this course. As a consequence of this hands-on activities, we talked to the Jose Rizal University's Guidance Testing Office. We will be making a project basing it on student feedback generated from school events under GTO with the use of Sentiment Analysis. The aim of the project is to increase student satisfaction in order to improve future event organization and participation. Even though I struggled to make it out alive due to the weekly activity leaving me no break from doing activities one after another, I am glad to say that I am still here! Thank you, Lord!
